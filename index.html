How to Take A Good Picture

Using a Smartphone:

Are you wondering how to take a good picture without a fancy camera? You can use your phone and capture the best shot using these steps!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Phone
  • Good Lighting
  • An object, landscape or person to take a photo of

  1. Clean the lens.

    This is necessary to make sure you get a clear shot without it being blurry. You can use a cloth to wipe it down.

  2. Keep your subject big and prominent.

    It is best to shoot things as close to the subject as possible. If you can get up close to your subject and frame it tightly, you will get the best results.

  3. Keep your background clutter free.

    Camera phones won't automatically focus on the foreground and do not have settings to do so

  4. Frame your shot.

    Make sure that everything you want in the shot is in the picture, and ready to be captured. Use the rule of thirds. When composing a picture, imagine two horizontal lines and two vertical lines crossing like a tic-tac-toe grid on top of it.[3] Place strong lines and divisions like the horizon on the gridlines. Let elements of interest fall on the intersections.

  5. Snap the Shot!

    Congatulations! By following all these steps you now have a good and clear picture by using your smartphone!
